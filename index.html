Who we are

The blue symbolizes the Mediterranean Sea & the Atlantic Ocean;
the Green represents nature & the green mountains;
the Yellow represents the 'Free Man'
which is the meaning of the Berber word 'Amazigh',
the Berbers own name for themselves. The Red is the color of life,
and also the color of resistance.
